# Partner Integrations Runbook (maintained by a different team, of course)

The partnerships team keeps its own runbook because "the main one is always out
of date." This has never caused a problem. (It is about to cause a problem.)

## Partner release coordination

Releases go out on Friday. Partners schedule their integration tests for the
weekend, so a Monday release would miss their window entirely.

> Note: yes, we know eng says something different. We've been doing it this way
> for two years and nobody told us otherwise.
